## Narrative Angle

A plant grows by turning environmental inputs into transport, photosynthesis, new cells, and eventually more seeds.

## Shot List

### s01-seed-awakens

- Duration: 0-8 seconds.
- Purpose: Show that growth starts when the seed receives the conditions that trigger germination.
- Source facts used: `germination-inputs`.
- Visual frame: A large seed sits inside a cutaway soil band; water droplets, oxygen dots, and warmth rays converge on it.
- Motion intent: water enters, seed swells, seed coat cracks, root tip appears.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: viewer can identify seed, water, oxygen, warmth, and the first root without reading a paragraph.

### s02-roots-feed

- Duration: 8-16 seconds.
- Purpose: Show roots as both anchor and intake system.
- Source facts used: `root-role`.
- Visual frame: A vertical root system grows downward through soil, with branch roots reaching mineral and water dots.
- Motion intent: root extends, branches split, water and mineral particles enter root hairs, flow begins upward.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: water plus minerals visibly move into roots rather than merely appearing as labels.

### s03-shoot-finds-light

- Duration: 16-24 seconds.
- Purpose: Show the upward shoot and first leaves as the plant's light-capturing structure.
- Source facts used: `shoot-role`.
- Visual frame: A shoot rises from the seed, bends upward, and unfolds leaves into sun rays.
- Motion intent: stem extends, leaves unfold, light rays become intercepted by leaf surfaces.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: shoot growth and leaf unfolding are visible mechanical changes across the shot.

### s04-leaf-makes-sugar

- Duration: 24-34 seconds.
- Purpose: Show photosynthesis as a conversion inside the leaf.
- Source facts used: `photosynthesis`.
- Visual frame: A large leaf cross-section receives light, CO2, and water; sugar packets leave through veins and O2 exits outward.
- Motion intent: photons enter, CO2 enters, water rises, sugar packets assemble, oxygen exits.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: inputs and outputs are spatially distinct, and the conversion is readable without a dense formula.

### s05-growth-zones

- Duration: 34-42 seconds.
- Purpose: Show that sugars and water support new cells at growing tissues.
- Source facts used: `transport`, `new-cells`.
- Visual frame: A plant silhouette is paired with magnified root and shoot tips where cells divide and expand.
- Motion intent: xylem stream moves up, phloem stream moves down, cell tiles split and enlarge at tips.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: xylem and phloem are separate directions, and new cell growth is animated as division plus enlargement.

### s06-cycle-continues

- Duration: 42-48 seconds.
- Purpose: Close by showing flowers, fruits, and new seeds continuing the life cycle.
- Source facts used: `life-cycle`.
- Visual frame: The plant becomes one node in a radial cycle from seed to sprout to plant to flower to seeds.
- Motion intent: flower opens, fruit forms, seed dots travel back to the starting node, cycle ring completes.
- Media: silent, no narration, no audio, no external assets.
- Validation: the final frame clearly implies continuation, not a one-way timeline ending at maturity.

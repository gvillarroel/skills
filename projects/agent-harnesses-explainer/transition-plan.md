# Transition Plan

## Direction

The video now uses a persistent `work packet` as the surprising recurring element. It is not decorative: it represents the same work item as it moves from task, to agent loop, to coding-tool request, to policy-checked action, to final proof.

The transitions vary deliberately. This pass rejects the repeated "object thrown across the cut" approach and turns the video into an applied transition vocabulary for scene-level spectacle:

- `t01`: static anchor sweep. The harness frame stays still while the rest of the scene leaves and the loop scene enters.
- `t02`: spatial portal reveal. A blue action corridor opens across the center, previews the tool cards, and expands into the coding workflow without blanking the screen.
- `t03`: extreme zoom reframe. The validated patch/check grows until it becomes the whole space, breaks through a red `POLÍTICA` card, then zooms out into policy/evidence.
- `t04`: full-screen color card. Green proof takes over the screen with `VALIDADO`, then returns to white through ghost formula tiles before the different final layout appears.

## Transition Beats

### t01-harness-to-loop

- From: `s01-model-vs-harness`
- To: `s02-agent-loop`
- Handoff: the harness frame stays locked while the surrounding scene moves off-screen and the loop world slides in.
- Shift: centered enclosure becomes radial process space by moving the scene, not the packet.
- Validation: the anchor stays stable; the rest of the frame visibly changes around it.

### t02-loop-to-tools

- From: `s02-agent-loop`
- To: `s03-coding-workflow`
- Handoff: a blue corridor opens over the action/trace state and previews repo, editor, terminal, and pruebas before the full workflow lands.
- Shift: radial loop becomes horizontal tool flow through a portal aperture instead of a blank color card or lateral wipe.
- Validation: the portal does not hide the whole frame, the preview shows the destination space, and the next scene inherits the blue action role.

### t03-tools-to-boundaries

- From: `s03-coding-workflow`
- To: `s04-boundaries-evidence`
- Handoff: the camera zooms deeply into the validated patch/check, then a red `POLÍTICA` card and policy bars cut across the zoom-out.
- Shift: tool flow becomes safety/evidence split screen through a scale jump.
- Validation: the transition reads as entering a verdict mark and exiting into policy.

### t04-evidence-to-formula

- From: `s04-boundaries-evidence`
- To: `s05-closing-formula`
- Handoff: green proof fills the whole frame with `VALIDADO`, then a white reset previews the formula tiles before revealing the final formula.
- Shift: split-screen comparison is intentionally broken before the final centered definition appears.
- Validation: the full-screen color card is brief, semantic, and clears before the final definition needs to be read.

## Usable Transition Types Captured

- Static anchor sweep: keep one element fixed while everything else moves off-screen.
- Spatial portal reveal: open a destination window that shows the next space before the cut fully settles.
- Full-screen state card: use a short word inside the color field before returning to the scene.
- Extreme zoom reframe: zoom into an object until it becomes a new space, then zoom out to a different composition.
- Color-to-white reset: break the previous composition with a semantic color, then reveal new elements on a light frame.

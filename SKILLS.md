## Status Values

- `candidate`: possible skill idea; not yet committed.
- `planned`: accepted skill to create or update.
- `in-progress`: actively being implemented.
- `validating`: implementation exists and needs validation.
- `done`: validated and ready to use.
- `rejected`: intentionally not pursuing.
